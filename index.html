About 40 Under 40

40 UNDER 40 is an Initiative by JCI Surat Pride to spotlight the TOP 40 Most Inspiring People of Surat City. It is a Project which will showcase the activities, contribution to society, personal achievements and vision of the nominees. We live in a world of Influencers and now is the time to Put light on people whose WORK IS WHAT INFLUENCES the mass.

40 UNDER 40 will bring forth the YOUTH. Putting the people under the age of 40 years is what makes it unique. Accomplishing a level before 40 years of age is a big achievement and we would like to acknowledge the efforts of those and SHOWCASE their STORIES

It is to RECOGNIZE, HONOUR and CREATE AN INSPIRATION out of those 40 people

MISSION

What we are working for?

TO PROMOTE AND BRING OUT THE REAL GEMS of Surat City and INSPIRE THE YOUTH through their SUCCESS STORIES.

VISION

What is our AIM?

About JCI

JCI (JUNIOR CHAMBER INTERNATIONAL), is a world wide federation of young leaders and entrepreneurs.

JCI India is a voluntary organization, membership based NGO working since 1949 for developing the leadership skills of young men and women for this country.

Each JCI member shares the belief that in order to create the lasting positive change, we must improve ourselves and the world around us.

JCI offers meetings, dynamic training sessions and projects that provide opportunities to learn achieve and inspire active citizenship, while building their experience as leaders.

MISSION

To Provide Development Opportunities that empower young people to create positive change.

VISION

To be the global leading network of YOUNG ACTIVE CITIZEN.

About JCI SURAT PRIDE

JCI SURAT PRIDE is the Largest Chapter of JCI in Gujarat. Founded in the year 2011, this Chapter has had a different essence to it since the very beginning. A chapter of 151+ members, we have been actively organizing and leading activities like Community Service projects, Business Development Activities, PR events, Training programs and many more. Even in the online platform, we have been constantly keeping our activities at pace and have impacted lives to a huge extent.

JCI Surat Pride aims to be the Largest Chapter of India and we wish to do that for our constant activities and projects.

We Aim at PROMOTING RIGHT IDEALOGY to DEVELOP and EMPOWER Youth
